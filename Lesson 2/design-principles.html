Proximity

Navy Federal Credit Union

navydeferal.org

In this example of the Navy Federal Credit Union website, you can appreciate the proper application of the design principle of proximity. They keep the information for the customer well distribute. Each text section is easy to navigate. Look how convenient is located the button that invited you to join to this Bank, and also the one for the actual customer for sign in.

White space

Walmart

walmart.com

In the Walmart website, you can navigate with the free sensation that everything is in the place you need it. The spaces between images, text, buttons are well defined, giving you great readability. The concept of order and space to do not overwhelm the user, in opposite with websites where the information is presented so crowded.

Contrast

Netflix

Netflix.com

I found the Netflix website very dynamic and attractive to the user. It combines properly the colors and typefaces giving the contrast that engages the user to navigate for the website. I consider a black background, like Netflix uses, very convenient. It allows so easily to use any other color over it and immediately attract the user to the relevant part that they want to emphasize.
